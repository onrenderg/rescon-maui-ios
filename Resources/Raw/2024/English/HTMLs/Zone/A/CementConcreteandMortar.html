Cement Concrete and Mortar

● Cement mortar is used in the construction of walls to bind bricks or blocks in position.

● Use Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC) for RCC work, plastering, and other structural applications.
● Use Portland or Portland Pozzolana Cement (43 grade) for making mortar for brick/block masonry.

● Use richer mortar (e.g., 1:5 or 1:4) for making corners and edges during plaster and for repair work.
● Use one part of cement with six parts of clean sand for making mortar for masonry work and plaster.

● Avoid mixing water with a large volume of mortar at once. Mortar/concrete must be placed within 30 minutes after adding water.
