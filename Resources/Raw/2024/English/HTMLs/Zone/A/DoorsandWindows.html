Doors and Windows

What are ideal sizes and locations of openings for doors and windows in a house?

The opening should be provided 600mm away from the internal surface of a wall, and the distance between openings should also be a minimum of 600mm.

For seismic resistance, the preferable width of a window is 600mm, though at lower elevations, it can be up to 900mm.

Recommended door and window positioning:

Door and window openings should be positioned at least 450mm away from wall corners or junctions. According to NBC 2016, the following are standard sizes:

(1) Door Openings:

  • Main door: 1000 × 2100 mm to 1200 × 2100 mm
  • Kitchen: 900 × 2100 mm
  • Toilet: 750 × 2100 mm

(2) Window Openings:

  • 1/8th of the floor area of a room in temperate and composite climates
  • 1/12th of the floor area of a room in cold climates
