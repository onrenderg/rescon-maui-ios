Planning the House Layout

Sample plan is given here. (For detailed floor plan, refer to the next page). Follow these steps when constructing your house yourself.

• Formulate a layout plan for your house, keeping in mind Planning and Safety Guidelines.
• Find out the plinth area rate of construction for a nearby house.
• Multiply the Plinth area by the plinth area rate to estimate the investment required for construction.
• If you wish to add another storey later, plan for stairs and a flat roof to serve as a future floor.
• Plan ground-floor services so the upper floor can connect to the existing service lines.
• Follow guidelines on site selection and preparation as previously discussed.

Step 1: Preparation of Layout

● Formulate a layout plan for your house using the sample provided.
● Find out the plinth area rate for nearby construction and multiply by your plinth area to estimate costs.
● If planning an additional storey, arrange for stairs and a flat roof to serve as the future upper floor.
● Plan ground-floor services so they connect easily to future upper-floor service lines.
