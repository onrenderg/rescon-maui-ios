Sand

Guidelines for Sand Usage and Storage

• Use sand obtained from clean rivers or approved pits.
• Store sand on a clean platform to avoid contamination with soil and dirt.
• Ensure the sand is clean, free from grass, sticks, soil, and other debris. If necessary, sieve or wash the sand to remove all impurities.
• A good quality dry sand does not soil your hands and falls freely from the hand.
• Always measure sand using a standard measuring box.
• The size of the sand particles should be larger than the grains of 'Sujee' but smaller than sugar crystals.
