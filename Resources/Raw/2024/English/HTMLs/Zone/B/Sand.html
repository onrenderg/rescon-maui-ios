Sand

• Use sand obtained from clean rivers or approved pits.
• Store sand on a clean platform so that soil and dirt do not enter into the sand.
• Use clean sand free from pieces of grass, sticks, soil, and other debris. If you find some of these, sieve or wash the sand to remove all impurities.
• A good quality dry sand does not soil your hands and falls freely from the hand.
• Always measure sand using a standard measuring box.

● The size of sand particles should be bigger than grains of ‘Sujee’ but not bigger than crystals of sugar.
